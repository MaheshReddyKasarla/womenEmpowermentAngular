The Time is NOW : Volunteer for Women Empowerment Program

Volunteer for women empowerment program offers a unique experience for volunteering to positively affect the lives of young women and girls in the rural areas of India. The program gives volunteers the opportunity to teach, inspire young females. The volunteer spend the afternoons teaching these young women English speaking, basic computer skills along with many other encouraging lessons like self defense, first aid etc. These positive interactions help build up the confidence in these young women.
